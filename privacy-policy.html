Privacy Policy

At Steal a Brainrot, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website and play our online games.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not require you to provide personal information to play our games.

2. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your gaming experience, remember preferences, and analyze site traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work properly.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Improve our games and website performance.
  • Provide support and respond to inquiries.
  • Analyze trends to offer a better gaming experience.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. Third-party services (like analytics or ads) may collect data separately—please check their privacy policies.

5. Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our games are intended for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with a revised date.
